I'm a PhD student in the [Computer Vision Lab](http://cvlab.cse.msu.edu/) at Michigan State University, where I am advised by [Dr. Xiaoming Liu](https://www.cse.msu.edu/~liuxm/index2.html). My research interests are in 3D Computer Vision, focusing on 3D reconstruction and generation.

I spent the Summer of 2022 in the Autonomous Perception Team at NVIDIA working with [Dr. Yang Zheng](https://www.linkedin.com/in/yang-zheng-42891863/) on Stereo Hazard Detection.

I received my Master of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor specializing in Computer Vision. During my Masters, I had the honor of working with [Dr. Stella X. Yu](https://web.eecs.umich.edu/~stellayu/). I received my Bachelor of Technology degree from IIT Gandhinagar majoring in Electrical Engineering and minoring in Computer Science.
