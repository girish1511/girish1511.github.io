About me

I'm a PhD student in the Computer Vision Lab at the Michigan State University where I am advised by Dr. Xiaoming Liu. My research interests are in the field of 3D Computer Vision focusing on 3D reconstruction and generation.

I spent the Summer of 2022 in the Autonomous Perception Team at NVIDIA working with Dr. Yang Zheng on Stereo Hazard Detection.

I received my Master of Science degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Michigan-Ann Arbor specilaizing in the field of Computer Vision. During my Masters, I had the honor of working with Dr. Stella X. Yu. I received my Bachelor of Technology degree from IIT Gandhinagar majoring in Electrical Engineering and minoring in Computer Science.
